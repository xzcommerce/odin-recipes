The Odin Cappuccino

Description

'Cappuccino' comes from Latin Caputium, later borrowed in German/Austrian and modified into Kapuziner. It is the diminutive form of cappuccio in Italian, meaning "hood" or something that covers the head, thus cappuccino literally means "small capuchin".

The coffee beverage has its name not from the hood but from the colour of the hooded robes worn by monks and nuns of the Capuchin order.[13] This colour is quite distinctive, and capuchin was a common description of the colour of red-brown in 17th century Europe.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Coffee Beans
  • Water
  • Coffee Machine

Steps

  1. Pour the milk and the water into the coffee machine
  2. Push the start button
  3. Wait until it starts making noise
  4. Your coffee is done!
